MEET THE TEAM

We're recent college graduates who fell in love with matcha long before we fell into adulthood. Between classes, late nights, and caffeine-fueled study sessions, we tried matcha everywhere—cafes, pop-ups, specialty shops, you name it. And somewhere along the way, we started asking the same question over and over:

Why are we paying $9 for a cup of matcha that’s barely bigger than our hand?

The economy is expensive enough. Good matcha shouldn't be. So we decided to do something about it.

Our Goal:

Create a space where matcha is affordable without compromise.

We don't cut corners

We use the same amount of matcha (if not more), source high-quality powder, and focus on balance, flavor, and care in every cup.

  • No watered-down drinks.
  • No shortcuts.
  • No "premium" up-charges for things that should
    already be standard.

Every drink is hand-whisked, the way matcha is meant to be made. And yes—if you want, we’re happy to show you. Feel free to ask for a video of your drink being made from start to finish.

Transparency matters to us.

This café exists for students, creatives, and anyone tired of overpaying for something they genuinely love

We’re here to make matcha accessible, intentional, and worth coming back for

every single time.
